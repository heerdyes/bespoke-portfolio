/sys/proj

./what

/sys/proj is primarily a community of technophiles, that seeks to reinvent everything. every invention is just one way of achieving its necessity. to really understand and appreciate something, we must (atleast) build/do it (y)ourselves.

./why

initially the aim was to set up a "training institution", but it appears that such institutions are symptoms of bigger problems. corporations are troublesome for too many reasons. i feel learning must not have profit as a motive. otherwise it gets reduced to yet another greed machine.
the more powerful a tool, the more careful and compassionate must be its wielder. technologists must be increasingly sensitive to this.
if /sys/proj is able to inspire young minds to the possibilities that technology presents, it will have achieved success.

./how

search for headquarters is in progress. a mini library, a desktop/laptop, whiteboard and markers will be set up almost immediately afterwards. since this is not a company, the community assumes ownership of /sys/proj, not some third-party proprietor.
