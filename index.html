Automata

Machines that surround us, contain us and are contained within us.
Machines, chemical, biological, ecological,
mathematical, electrical, acoustic,
optical, hydraulic, pneumatic, psychological
A universe of machines, we perpetually find ourselves in.

Waveband

A set of frequencies employed to communicate
in this case, between families of automata
so that they may cause the emergence of complex systems,
who in turn might function as higher-order, automata.

Questions

Why? What? etc.
Automata follow grammars and seed the emergence of fractals.
Simple automata communicate with each other to evolve complex systems.
They communicate through frequencies and evolve ways of navigating and manipulating such wavebands.
It is as if the world comes into existence through recursion, or, self-reflection.
